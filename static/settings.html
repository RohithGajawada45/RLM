Where do I get these?

In the Azure Portal, open your Azure OpenAI resource → Keys and Endpoint for AZURE_ENDPOINT / AZURE_API_KEY, and Model deployments for your deployment names. You'll need one chat-completion deployment (e.g. gpt-5-mini) and one embeddings deployment (e.g. text-embedding-3-small). Your key is only used to call your own Azure resource on your behalf — it is never persisted to disk and is dropped from memory after a few hours of inactivity or when you click Disconnect.
